Our History

Mid-19th century England. The firm of George Townsend & Co., flitting from one opportunity to another, chanced upon the pedal-cycle trade.Little did they know they were on the cusp of something that would last over a century and longer. Soon, George Townsend & Co. were manufacturing their own brand of bicycles.

In 1893, they became the Enfield Manufacturing Company Limited, accompanied by the trademark 'Made Like a Gun.'And so, a legendary brand was born.

Become a Dealer

At Royal Enfield, we look to partner with dealers that enable consumers to start their journey of exploration.
